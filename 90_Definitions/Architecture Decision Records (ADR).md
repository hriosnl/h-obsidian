The purpose of ADRs is **memory and clarity**.

They explain _why_ the system is the way it is, not just _what_ it is.

This helps to understand past tradeoffs without relying on oral history or guesswork.

ADRs preserve **reason**, not just outcomes.

ADRs are not just architectural memory; they are **governance artifacts** for CC’s self-directed evolution.

Each ADR captures a single architectural decision together with the context, reasoning, and consequences necessary to understand and evaluate that decision in the future.
