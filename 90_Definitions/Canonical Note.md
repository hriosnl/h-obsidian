A note stored under `100_Canonical`.
It represents authoritative, long-lived knowledge.
Its content is treated as higher priority than Active or Legacy notes.
Canonical Notes are edited deliberately and infrequently.

Canonical Notes are the **final form of thought** in your system.
They are not where thinking happens. They are where thinking **ends**.

A Canonical Note represents something you are willing to treat as _true, stable, and reusable_ across time.
