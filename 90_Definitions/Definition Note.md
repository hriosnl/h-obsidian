A note stored under `90_Definitions`.  
It contains stable semantic anchors that define the precise meaning of terms used across the system.  
Definitions clarify language and prevent conceptual drift, but do not prescribe behavior or enforce structure.  
Notes in this directory are authoritative in meaning, edited infrequently, and referenced broadly.  
When a definition begins to impose constraints or govern behavior, it no longer belongs here and must be promoted to Canonical as a rule.
