The next evolution is not “AI versions” of those companies. It is a different axis entirely.

Unix → startups mapped **commands → services**.  
AI maps **intent → outcome**.

Below are the true successors, framed as primitives, not products. Each line is one evolutionary jump, with several concrete idea shapes.

---

Search (`grep` → Google)  
Next: **Truth synthesis**

- Ask a question and receive a structured answer that explains itself, cites sources, and adapts as facts change.
    
- Continuous beliefs that update over time instead of static search results.
    
- Personal truth layers: the same question answered differently based on your goals, risk tolerance, or context.
    

---

File sync (`rsync` → Dropbox)  
🤔 Next: **State continuity**

- You never manage files; your work state follows you across devices and moments.
    
- Thought-level versioning: not “files,” but decisions, drafts, and intentions are synced.
    
- Roll back _meaning_, not bytes.
    

---

Scheduling (`cron`)  
Next: **Autonomous time allocation**

- You express outcomes; the system rearranges time automatically.
    
- Deadlines negotiate with each other.
    
- Time becomes elastic, not blocked.
    

---

Monitoring (`top`, `ps` → Datadog)  
🤔 Next: **Self-explaining systems**

- Systems narrate what they are doing and why.
    
- Failures are explained before they are noticed.
    
- Monitoring shifts from watching metrics to understanding behavior.
    

---

Version control (`diff` → GitHub)  
🤔 Next: **Decision lineage**

- Every choice has a traceable history: why it was made, what alternatives existed, what changed.
    
- Teams fork _decisions_, not code.
    
- Undo regret, not commits.
    

---

Remote access (`ssh` → cloud platforms)  
Next: **Capability delegation**

🤔 - You don’t access machines; you delegate intent.
    
- “Make this true” replaces “log into this box.”
    
🤔 - Resources assemble themselves temporarily to fulfill a request, then disappear.
    

---

Build systems (`make`, CI)  
Next: **Outcome compilation**

- You describe what success looks like; the system figures out the steps.
    
- Pipelines are inferred, not written.
    
🤔 - Failure produces revised plans, not error logs.
    

---

Email / messaging (`mail`)  [[Email Evolution - Intent Negotiation]]

🤔 Next: **Intent negotiation**

🤔 - Messages are replaced by goals and constraints.
    
🤔 - Back-and-forth disappears; alignment happens instantly.
    
🤔 - Communication shifts from words to shared understanding.
    

---

Databases  
Next: **Living memory**

- Data reorganizes itself based on use.
    
- Queries change the structure.
    
- Memory becomes active, not stored.
    

---

UI / dashboards  
Next: **Disappearing interfaces**

- No menus, no flows, no screens as the primary object.
    
- The interface is conversation, gesture, or silence.
    
- Visibility only appears when uncertainty exists.
    

---

The unifying shift:

Old software answered: _“Which tool do you want?”_  
SaaS answered: _“Which workflow fits you?”_  
AI answers: _“What do you want to be true?”_

That is why SaaS feels outdated to you. It still lives one layer too low.
