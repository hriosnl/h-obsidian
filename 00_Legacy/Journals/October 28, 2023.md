# Nefer
The Egyptian hieroglyph for "perfect, complete" (with the extended meanings of "good, pleasant, well, beautiful") in Gardiner's sign list is numbered F35; its phonetic value is nfr, with a reconstructed pronunciation of nafir and a conventional Egyptological vocalization of nefer.
## NFR
Broadly, functional requirements define what a system is supposed to _do_ and non-functional requirements define how a system is *supposed to __be__.*
