Your brain is not *reacting* to the world, but it is always *predicting*.

Emotions are you brain's constructions of what your bodily sensation means in a specific context. 

Physical changes in your body (heart rate, breathing, increase in cortisol) *have no inherent psychological meaning.*

Everything you see, hear, *sense*, is a combination of what's out there in the world and what's in your head.
- [I] So, this also mean that you can change your perception by:
	- updating what you know or what's in your head and
	- updating/choosing what you want to see outside
