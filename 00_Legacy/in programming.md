## Self-help
- Just learn the fundamentals and let AI and hands-on experience teach you about the complexities i.e. patterns
- You need to **produce** and not just *consume*
- The most complex software are built with the simplest concepts, so technically creating such complex system is just a matter of speed and not intelligence
	- that is why you need to [[build something you love| build something you like, or better, something you love]] so that you won't easily lose motivation
- when you are [[tranquility is a superpower|relaxed and at ease]], programming concepts become easy to understand
- Let AI (with guidance, or read-check) do everything, if it failed at some point then it’s great for you will have a further experiment for the capability of your AI i.e. debugging your whole codebase
- do not be afraid on reading others' code: you just *don't know it **yet***
