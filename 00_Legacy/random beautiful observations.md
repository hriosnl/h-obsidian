A spider here at our garden spends so much time just sleeping calmly on a leaf
Today, I found that he made his web (possibly to hunt for food)
I put a leaf on it web and poked it several times as to pretend that there's a catch
After a few pokes, he immediately (automatically!) went directly to the spot of the web where the leaf is
I moved away, and went I looked back I saw him removing the leaf and throwing it away from his web 😆
I decided to search a food for him to retry the beautiful scene again but failed, so just resorted to putting the same leaf and poked it again
This time, after several poked, the spider did not move
Did he knew that it's the same? That it was just a leaf?
Or, more possibly, did he just intrinsically knew probability? That to save his energy, he just need to go there at specific interval (depending on basic factor such as level of hunger?)
