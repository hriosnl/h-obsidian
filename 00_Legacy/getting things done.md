1.  Define what to do
2.  Deconstruct until you reach something doable with just a few moves/decisions
3.  Put those *doable* in your tasks board
4.  Pick one thing to do
5.  Know when to **stop**
	-  Done? Celebrate your small win. 🏆
	-  Blocked? Note the obstacles. Get another *doable* to do. Repeat #4.
