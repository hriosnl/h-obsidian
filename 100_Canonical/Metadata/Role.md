**role**  
Declares the functional intent of a note.

- **reference**  
	Pointers, quotes, summaries of external material. Not assertions.
	For AI → Never treat as belief or instruction.

- **decision**  
	A concluded choice. Treated as authoritative unless superseded.
	For AI → Apply as a constraint but can be easily challenged.

- **rule**  
	Normative instruction or constraint. Used for evaluation and enforcement.
	For AI → Apply as a constraint when generating or evaluating other notes.

- **journal**
	First-person observation, reflection, or inner state. Subjective and non-analytical.  
	For AI → Read-only context. Do not modify, merge, summarize, reinterpret, or challenge unless explicitly instructed.
