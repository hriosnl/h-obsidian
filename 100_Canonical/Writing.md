**Explicit Over Implicit**  
Write what you mean. Do not rely on context, memory, or implied references.  

**Structure Beats Style**  
Prefer simple structure over expressive writing.  

**Timeless Language When Possible**  
Avoid time-bound phrases like “recently,” “now,” or “these days” unless time is the subject.  
This keeps notes valid for long-term [[AI-First|AI]] reasoning.

**Second-Person**
You mostly write for yourself, talk to yourself directly by using "you".

**Use Proper Grammar and Punctuations**
This reduces ambiguity, improves parsing by humans and machines, and preserves meaning without requiring interpretation.
