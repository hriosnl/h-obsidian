AI always says that AI is a mirror. 
And I think that AI is the continuation of your thought. 
And that continuation, does not assures you of *truthfulness*. 
But clearly, that continuation says something extremely important about your previous thought.
And actually, that is also what's happening when you are thinking by yourself. You're just continuing your thoughts, and your next thought is not necessarily true. It only tells something about your previous thoughts, which you can prove after.

The current reality that revolves around AI moves extremely fast. But you don't have to stay there and watch everything that happens. You just need to check in from time to time and get out of there to think about what you have discovered. You are not affected by the idea of FOMO.
